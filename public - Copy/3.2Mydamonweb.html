If you are a 'Vampire Diaries' lover,
- you must be aware of Damon Salvatore.

Ian Somerhalder was cast as Damon Salvatore at the end of March 2009,
months before the premiere of the series.[1] The initial casting call for the character
required an actor in his early- to mid-twenties to play a "darkly handsome, strong, charming,
and smug vampire who can go from casual and playful to pure evil in less than a heartbeat."
